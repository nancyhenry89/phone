Rational Marketing LTD

Purpose

To manage the participation of the interested party in draws and/or promotions organised by Rational Marketing LTD. / The commercial use of the database through the sending of commercial communications of third parties or the carrying out of market studies. / To communicate the data of the interested parties to companies, organizations and/or entities interested in directing advertising towards the said interested parties.

Legitimization

To manage the participation of the interested party in draws and/or promotions organised by Rational Marketing LTD - Fulfiiling the contract./ Carrying out of third party advertising campaigns/ market studies by Rational Marketing LTD - Consent of the interested party./ Communication of the data to carry out advertising campaigns to companies, organizations and/or entities interested in carrying out these actions / market studies - Consent of the interested party.

Recipients

Points of sale of products / draw prizes and/or gift products / Advertisers / Marketing / Call Centre Companies. Sponsors of the draw.

Rights

Regarding their data, the interested party will have the right to:

Access it.

Delete or rectify it.

Restrict its processing.

Request its portability.

Oppose it being processed.
